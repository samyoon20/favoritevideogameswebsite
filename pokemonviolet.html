Pokemon Violet

About Pokemon Violet

Discover the new world of Pokemon Violet, an exciting adventure filled with unique creatures and thrilling challenges.

Lucario

Lucario is from the fourth generation pokemon video game series, Pokemon Diamond and Pearl Lucario is based on the Egyptian god, Anubis, and has the power to detect their opponent's thoughts and actions. Lucario is the second evolution form of Riolu.

Charizard

Charizard is one of the original pokemon and comes from the first edition of pokemon video games, Pokemon Red and Blue. Charizard is a fire-breathing dragon and is the last evolved form of Charmander. Charizard is one of the most popular pokemon to this day.

Garatina

Garatina, a legendary pokemon comes from the third installment of pokemon video games, Pokemon Platinum. A personal favorite and James' favorite legendary pokemon. Garatina lives in a dimension known as the Distortion World and is also called the Renegade Pokemon.

Miraidon

Miraidon is the front cover and legendary pokemon of Pokemon Violet, the ninth generation of pokemon video games. Miraidon is known as the Iron Serpent and is ancestry roots back to Cyclizar, a Dragon type Pokemon.

Koraidon

Koraidon is another legendary pokemon in the Pokemon Violet game, however Koraidon is in the front cover of Pokemon Scarlet. Scarlet is also in the ninth generation of pokemon video games. Koraidon is known as the Winged King and is also ancestor of Cyclizar too.

Sprigatito

Sprigatito is one of the three pokemon that the video game allows the user to choose to pick as the trainer's very first pokemon. Sprigatito is a grass-type pokemon and has a cat-like body with leaf-like tufts on its neck. James chose this pokemon as his starter pokemon in the video game Pokemon Violet!
